# Peg Defense

## Scenario A: Normal Conditions

- tick stays near peg
- low fee
- no hard limits hit

## Scenario B: Soft Depeg

- moderate deviation
- fee increases
- soft caps reduce toxic flow size

## Scenario C: Hard Depeg

- severe deviation or proxy threshold trigger
- highest fee
- strict size/impact constraints
- optional cooldown prevents burst toxicity

## Mitigation Summary

- toxic execution dampening: higher fee + strict caps
- flip-flop suppression: hysteresis
- governance risk reduction: timelock + update intervals
